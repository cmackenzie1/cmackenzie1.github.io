About Me

July 27, 2018

Hey there! It looks like you found my website. Well since you made it this far, here is a little bit about myself.

My name is Cole Mackenzie, and I am a studying Computing Science at the Univeristy of Alberta in Edmonton, Alberta.

My favorite programming languages are Go, Java, and Python sprinkled with a little Ruby for Chef. I used to be a long time Android fan, but have finally made the switch to the Apple ecosystem. Apple Watch, iPhone, iPad, Macbook - I’ve got them all.

As part of my B.Sc, I got a job with Intuit in their Edmonton office where I joined the CTO Data Extraction team for 16-months working on quite a few projects. The first was a “next gen” OCR platform for semi structured data that used a variety of methods, such as spatial awareness, digital fingerprinting, and computer vision. I quickly worked my way in the “DevOps” space - creating a complete CI/CD pipeline for our application, automating the infrastructure with infrastructure as code, and helped develop a common pattern for Blue/Green deployments in AWS that is used across several other teams.

After my 16-month Co-op I visited my new favorite city, Seattle, then moved to San Jose, California to to do an internship with Intuit at their Mountain View Campus. Just check out this picture of the city I caputred!
